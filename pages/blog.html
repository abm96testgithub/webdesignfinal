Wine & Cheese Tasting

On Friday, April 13th, 2018 we held a French Wine And Cheese tasting at our headquaters in SoHo, NY. All of our served wines and cheeses were carefully matched together by our cusine experts. Each guest in attendance received score cards to rate each wine and each cheese they tried throughout the day, allowing them to track their favorites. All wines and cheeses served at the event were available for purchase and/or order at the end of the tasting.

The Pairings:

  • Beaujolais & Brie De Meaux
  • Chenin Blanc & Camebert De Normandie
  • Fumé Blanc & Crottin De Cavignol
  • Prosecco & Fourme D' D'Ambert
  • Cabernet Sauvignon & Ossau-Iraty
  • Chardonnay & Pont-L'Évêque
  • Tokay Pinot Gris & Tomme De Savoie
  • Riesling & Vacherin Mont D'or
